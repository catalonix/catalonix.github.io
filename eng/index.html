About Catalonix
Creating meaningful change — for a greener future and for all of us.
Catalonix Inc. advances sustainable futures through creative thinking and continuous innovation.
Our company delivers data-driven insights and solutions across urban, environmental, agricultural, and forestry sectors — transforming complex challenges into actionable knowledge.
Since our founding, Catalonix has developed core technologies such as metaverse-based aggregation of distributed big data, urban environmental reconstruction frameworks, and 3D traffic–environment city modeling.
In environmental fields, we build integrated knowledge platforms supporting sustainable growth across crop cultivation, marine and port environments, and forest ecosystems.
